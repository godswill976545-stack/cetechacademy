# SHAPE: The Void Mastery Re-Theme
Target: Whole Website (index.html, styles, global theme)

## 1. Visual Direction: "The Digital Vault"
Shift from "Clinical White SaaS" to "Elite Futuristic Command Center". The site should feel like a high-end, immersive digital experience—dark, deep, and pulsing with energy.

## 2. Color Strategy: Drenched (Deep Space)
- **Primary Background**: `#070b10` (Deep Space Blue). No more `bg-white`.
- **Surface/Panels**: Glassmorphism.
  - Background: `rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.6)` with 20px blur.
  - Border: `rgba(0, 123, 255, 0.2)` (Electric Blue 20% opacity).
  - Inner Glow: 1px solid light-blue stroke to define edges.
- **Accents**: 
  - Electric Blue (`#007bff`) and Cyan (`#00d2ff`) used as light sources.
  - Text Gradients: `linear-gradient(to bottom right, #fff, #00d2ff)`.
- **Neutrals**:
  - Primary Text: `#f8fafc` (Slate 50).
  - Secondary Text: `#94a3b8` (Slate 400).

## 3. Typography & Hierarchy
- **Headings**: Space Grotesk. Increase weight to Bold/ExtraBold. Use a "glowing" text effect for the H1.
- **Body**: Inter. Slightly increase line-height for readability against dark backgrounds.
- **Scale**: Steeper contrast between H1 (massive, cinematic) and body text.

## 4. Layout & Tactility
- **Spacing**: Increase section padding (py-32 -> py-48) to give the design more "air" and prestige.
- **Cards**: Replace generic glass panels with "Depth Panels"—semi-transparent dark backgrounds with high-contrast electric blue borders on hover.
- **Aurora Background**: Keep the aurora but remove the `bg-white/40` wash. Let the deep colors shine through.

## 5. Motion
- **Ambient**: Keep `float` and `pulse-glow`, but make them feel like neon lights reflecting off the surfaces.
- **Entrance**: Keep `slide-up` but add a subtle fade-in-from-scale effect.

## 6. AI Slop Test
- **Reject**: Generic dark-mode (just #000 and #fff).
- **Reject**: "Neon-on-black" gamer aesthetic (too loud).
- **Aim**: "Apple-esque" precision combined with "Cyberpunk" depth. Professional, elite, and polished.
